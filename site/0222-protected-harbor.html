Protected Harbor

1CommerceCommerceJob Kind Trade

Description

This harbor, set up in a particularly well-chosen location, is, for nations with a maritime tradition, a better alternative to the Small Harbor. It is far superior to it, as it can produce warships and generate more income, while maintaining at all times a sizable garrison of troops.
