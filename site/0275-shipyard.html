Shipyard

2CommerceCommerceJob Kind Trade

Description

Shipyards are specialized structures where a lot of ships can be produced in an efficient way. Transport ships, galleys, galleass, roundships and many more types will benefit from the facility provided here. If you consider serious sea operations or want to contest maritime supremacy with another nation, they are a must-have.
