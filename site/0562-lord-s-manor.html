Lord's Manor

2StewardshipStewardshipJob Kind Govern

Description

This structure is pivotal in developing Stewardship and National Authority in a region as it is mandatory to access the second part of Tier II Stewardship structures. This Manor is a large estate owned by a nobleman. Not quite as big and impressive as a Paramount Lord Manor (from French Par à mont) it is big enough to host many rooms with dedicated purposes, such as a great hall, a chapel and a solar (private room of the lord).
