Iqta' Land Grant

1AgricultureAgricultural

Description

The Iqta' Land Grant is the Muslim equivalent to the Manorial Demesne, but with a twist: Unlike the European feudal system, the Iqta' was not hereditary. This discouraged investment and maintenance, so there is a 10% (Minus Ruler Administration) chance per turn that an Iqta' Land Grant degrades to a less productive domain (EVENT). But once in a while, with a change of owner, investments will be made and productivity will be restored (EVENT: 5% chance plus Ruler Administration to be fully restored if in the last stage).
