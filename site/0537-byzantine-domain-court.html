Byzantine Domain Court

1StewardshipStewardshipJob Kind Govern

Description

Here a Byzantine Strategos will administer and supervise the daily domestic affairs of several regions, which are grouped together into a 'theme' (equivalent to a Province). As also a military commander, he will have at his disposal thematic troops, represented by a strong garrison. The overlord can optionnally appoint a Peer of the Realm acting as governor of the theme, providing extra bonuses, depending on the character in charge.
