Siege Workshop

2MilitaryMilitary

Description

Having a siege fabrication workshop on site is of great help to a stronghold, as it allows the maintenance of expert engineers of a large group of Siege Engines (it will provide extra Siege Engines in case of siege). The workshop uses a great deal of Wood, but you'll also need either Tar or Stone as a bonus for the structure to be buildable.
