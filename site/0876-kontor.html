Kontor

2CommerceCommerceJob Kind Trade

Description

This Kontor (equivalent to a Large Protected Harbor) reflects a nation's recognition of Hanseatic privileges and acceptance of their commercial presence. Compared to the standard Large Harbor, it offers enhanced trade capacity and maintains a permanent garrison capable of repelling medium-scale threats. As with other major harbors, it produces Transport Ship goods. This special Trade Good determines the quality and speed of your troop transports. It also provides a high Siege Resist value, causing any siege to last a very long time unless the harbor is effectively blockaded by enemy fleets.
