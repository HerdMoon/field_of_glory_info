Grad

2MilitaryMilitary

Description

The Grad is an early Medieval fortified town in Slavonic countries (Gorod is the specific Russian designation, but it fills the same function). It features a massive wall often earth-filled with material taken from the exterior ditch and compacted then constrained by stone or timbers. This is a very resistant construction given its thickness, although the wall formed is seldomly very high. Within it live the population and the local noble.
