Wishing Well

2InfrastructureJob Kind HealthJob Kind Healthdetail

Description

This rare upgrade of a Well doesn't deliver much more water and will prevent the upgrade to a Deep Well. EVENT: The building once in a while (when the commoners throw a piece or two) will generate 'luck', an unfathomable resource that will be used automatically to avoid character's fate events, death in battles and assassination attempts. Weight carefully the choice of not building this structure, as this is a rare proposal.
